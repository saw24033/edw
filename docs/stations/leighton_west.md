# Leighton West Station

## Overview
**Leighton West** is a small terminus station on the Leighton West Branch, located between Leighton City and Edgemead. It serves as a terminal point for Stepford Connect services.

- **Station Code:** LTW
- **Station Type:** Terminus
- **Operator:** Stepford Connect
- **Signal Zone:** D
- **Headcode:** K
- **Electrification:** Yes
- **Accessibility:** Level access (step-free)

## Key Features

1. **Single Platform Operation:** Only Platform 1 is in active use, with a single track serving the station
2. **Terminus Station:** All trains terminate here, with services running via either Leighton City or Edgemead
3. **Historical Platform:** A disused second platform suggests the station originally had higher capacity
4. **Triangle Junction:** The line forms a double-track triangle from Leighton City and Edgemead before merging to single track
5. **Passing Loop:** Despite being mostly single track, there is a passing loop on the branch to allow trains to pass

## Notable Information

- Based on **Colchester Town station** in Essex, England
- One of three stations with only one active platform (along with Airport Terminal 1 and Carnalea Bridge)
- One of five Stepford Connect stations without a direct route to Stepford Central
- The only station in Zone D without routes to stations in the Stepford Area
- Despite having 5 routes terminating here, two routes (R041 and R043) only serve nearby Edgemead and Leighton City respectively
- The station has a wheelchair accessible symbol indicating step-free access
